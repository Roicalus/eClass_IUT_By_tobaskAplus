Privacy Policy

App: eclassiut

Last updated: 2026-03-25

Overview

eclassiut is an attendance and classroom-management application. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and the third-party services involved.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: when you sign in, we may process your name, email address, and account identifier provided by your sign-in method.
  • Attendance and class data: student names/IDs you enter, lessons, groups, and attendance marks.
  • Usage data: basic analytics and diagnostic information (if enabled) to understand app performance and usage.

How We Use Data

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your data.
  • To store and sync your lessons/attendance across devices.
  • To generate an attendance export to Google Sheets when you choose Save & Send.
  • To improve reliability and performance.

Google Sheets Export

If you use Save & Send, the app requests permission to create and update Google Sheets in your Google account. The exported spreadsheet is created in your Google Drive under your account. You can delete the spreadsheet at any time from Google Drive.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In (to authenticate and request Google Sheets access, when used).
  • Firebase Authentication (user authentication).
  • Cloud Firestore (data storage and sync).
  • Firebase Analytics (optional usage analytics).
  • Firebase Storage (if you upload or store files within the app).

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only as necessary to provide the service (for example, with Firebase as the hosting/auth/database provider, and with Google APIs when you export to Sheets).

Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account remains active or until you delete it. If you need help deleting your data, contact us.

Your Choices

  • You can stop using Google Sheets export at any time.
  • You can revoke the app’s access to Google in your Google Account settings.
  • You can request deletion of your app data by contacting us.
